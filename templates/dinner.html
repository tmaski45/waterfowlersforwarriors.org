Wild Game Dinner

 Saturday, April 19th 2014
 1800 E. Parish Rd. Kawkawlin, MI 48631

Come enjoy a fun filled night at the Kawkawlin Twp. Hall with fellow sportsman and pay homage to this great country and the fine men and wormen that protect it and us. Dinner will be prepared by Executive Chef Jeff Langer, Chef John Gorm and Chef Dan Kiley. Menu to include Venison Meat Balls, Alligator, Cajun Perch, Rabbit, Broiled Ham, Prime Rib, White Fish Perogis, Smoked Duck Breast Perogis, also 2 drink tickets with additional tickets available. Top three door prizes from dinner ticket will be 1st patriotic AR-15, 2nd Winchester SX3, Tikka T3 30-06 and lost of other door prizes. 50/50 raffles card gun drawings doard gun drawing muzzle loaders, crossbow and more.

Dinner tickets are $50.00 and will be limited. To purchase a ticket you must contact Scott Fowler at (989) 233-3449.
